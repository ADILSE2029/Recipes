Daal Mash

Description

Daal mash is a traditional Pakistani lentil dish made using split white urad lentils, known for its creamy texture and mild, comforting flavor. It's typically tempered with garlic, cumin, and dried red chilies, making it a simple, everyday dish often paired with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white urad daal (mash), washed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. Boil the daal with turmeric, salt, and water until soft and creamy.
  2. In a separate pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, garlic, and dried red chilies.
  3. Fry until the garlic turns golden and fragrant.
  4. Pour this tempering over the boiled daal.
  5. Mix well and simmer for a few more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice or roti.
